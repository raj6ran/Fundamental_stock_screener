## Why These 9

### 1. BLS International (BLS.NS) — Score 83.1, GEM
- **Thesis**: Dominant visa & consular services franchise. Capital-light business with 37% revenue CAGR.
- **Moat**: Wide — regulatory licenses, switching costs, distribution network across 66 countries.
- **Frameworks**: 10/10 passed. Every published framework picks this — value, growth, quality, momentum.
- **Key numbers**: ROCE 27.3%, ROE 29.4%, D/E 0.21, Altman Z 11.75, FCF conversion 1.31.
- **Risk**: Concentration in visa processing; geopolitical disruption.

### 2. Natco Pharma (NATCOPHARM.NS) — Score 73.8, STRONG
- **Thesis**: Deeply undervalued pharma at PE 12.9 with 30% ROCE and 32% revenue CAGR. Market hasn't priced the growth.
- **Moat**: Wide — generic drug pipeline, Para IV filings, cost advantage in API manufacturing.
- **Frameworks**: 10/10 passed.
- **Key numbers**: ROCE 30.0%, D/E 0.04, Piotroski 7/9, Altman Z 14.82.
- **Risk**: Drug pricing regulation, patent cliff timing, FDA inspection outcomes.
